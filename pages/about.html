Who We Are

We Are a
Musical Family

Grace Tunes Musical School is a beacon of music education in Kiambu, Kenya — known for enriching the lives of students through the transformative power of music. We are not just a school; we are a musical family that nurtures talent and fosters creativity in every student we meet.

Our story began with a deep passion for music and the desire to share its beauty with as many young people as possible. Founded by committed musicians and experienced educators, we bring musical artistry and academic excellence together under one roof.

We serve schools, families, and individuals across Kiambu — offering everything from one-on-one instrument lessons to full orchestral programmes, DJ classes, ballet, and music production.

Our Vision

A Kenya where every child's unique musical voice is honoured, and where music crosses cultural barriers to create harmony and unity.

Our Mission

To equip students with the musical knowledge, skills, and confidence they need to thrive — in the classroom, on stage, and in life.

Our Values

Excellence, creativity, inclusivity, patience, and a genuine passion for the transformative power of music in every child's life.

Our Community

We partner with schools, churches, and families across Kiambu — building a lasting musical legacy, one note at a time.

Our Journey

How We Grew

Every great school has a story. Ours is one of passion, perseverance, and a belief that music can change a child's life forever.

The Beginning
Grace Tunes Musical School Is Founded

Started with a small group of students and a big dream — to bring quality music education to every child in Kiambu.

Early Growth
First School Partnerships

We began partnering with local primary schools, bringing in-school music programmes aligned with CBC and Montessori curricula.

Expanding
ABRSM Examination Preparation

Grace Tunes became a recognised ABRSM preparation centre, with students achieving grades from 1 through 8 across multiple instruments.

Today
12+ Instruments · 14+ Classes

A full-service music school offering everything from piano and voice to DJ, ballet, and music production — for students of all ages.
