Our Programmes

A Path for Every Student

Each programme is thoughtfully designed to meet students exactly where they are — and take them further than they imagined possible.

01

Customised Classroom Programmes

Designed to enhance academic and musical development, tailored to each school's unique needs and curriculum framework — CBC, Montessori, 8-4-4, British, and more.

02

Instrument Mastery Track

A structured progression through beginner, intermediate, and advanced levels on any of our 12+ instruments. Students build technique, musicianship, and performance confidence at their own pace.

03

ABRSM Examination Preparation

Targeted lessons guiding students from Grade 1 through Grade 8, with mock examinations, practice materials, and expert mentorship at every stage of the journey.

04

Ensemble & Marching Band

Dynamic group performance programmes — choir, orchestra, and marching band — that build teamwork, school pride, and real-world performance experience.

05

Holiday Intensive Programmes

Fast-track programmes during April, August, and December holidays — giving students focused progress in a structured and energising short-course format.

06

Christmas Cantata Productions

A beloved annual tradition — full seasonal performances that bring students, families, and the community together in celebration of music and the festive season.

07

Modern Music Electives

DJ performance, music production, and ballet — contemporary electives that expand the school's musical offering and attract students with diverse creative interests.

08

Adult Learning Programme

Flexible evening and weekend classes designed specifically for adult learners — structured, encouraging, and adapted to adult learning styles and schedules.

Who We Serve

Programmes for Every School

Whether you run a nursery, primary, secondary, or special school — we have a programme designed precisely for your students and curriculum.

🌱

Early Childhood

Playful, age-appropriate music introduction for nursery and pre-primary students using rhythm, song, and movement.

🏫

Primary Schools

Full in-school programmes including classroom music, recorder, choir, and instrument lessons aligned with CBC and Montessori.

🎓

Secondary Schools

Advanced instrument tuition, music theory, ABRSM preparation, marching band, orchestra, and music production electives.

💼

International Schools

British curriculum-aligned programmes with ABRSM examination pathways and extracurricular music enrichment.

🏠

Home Learners

Flexible private one-on-one or small group lessons for homeschooled students at our premises or delivered at home.

🤝

Community Programmes

Church choirs, community orchestras, and holiday music camps open to all students in the Kiambu community.
