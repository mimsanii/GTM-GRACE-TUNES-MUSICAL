Our Story

We are a
Musical Family

Grace Tunes Musical is a beacon of musical education, known for enriching lives through the power of music. We are more than a school — we are a musical family, nurturing talent and fostering creativity in every student we meet.

Our story began with a strong passion for music and the desire to spread its charm to as many people as possible. Founded by committed musicians and educators, we bring musical artistry and academic brilliance together.

Vision

Every student's distinctive melody will be honored, and music will cross cultural barriers to promote harmony and unity.

Mission

To equip students with knowledge and abilities they need in music, fostering an environment where every talent can blossom.

What We Deliver

Scope of
Services

Music is the performer, and education is the stage. Our programs are meticulously planned to perfectly mesh with school curricula — making music essential for every child's journey.

🏫

Classroom Sessions

Adaptable programs preparing students for ABRSM examinations, aligned with CBC, Montessori, and 844 education systems. Recorder classes that are genuinely engaging and fun for every child.

🎸

Instrumental Lessons

02
